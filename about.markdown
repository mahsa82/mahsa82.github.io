Although my hands-on experience has been primarily with Django and Django REST Framework (DRF), I find Linux fascinating — to me, it feels like a powerful workspace where you have the freedom to do anything you want. Every new technology I learn opens a door to new possibilities, and that excites me. This passion pushes me to keep learning, stay curious, and never settle for "good enough." At the end of the day, building things that work — and work well — is what truly makes me happy.

I'm currently looking for work that utilizes my expertise in Django and Django REST Framework along with my Linux knowledge, and allows me to grow as a backend developer in Python.
